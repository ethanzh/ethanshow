Photos from 23 April 2000

(click on a photo to see a full-size image, or here to see them all on one page)

We spent Easter 2000 with our friends Barbara and Mark Norris in Aberdeen, Scotland. We woke up to sunshine and blue skies, which is not guaranteed in this part of the world. Breakfast of soft-boiled eggs and hot cross buns in the sunroom got our day started. Then, after the photo shoot, we all drove north to the town of Sandend to Alex and Christine's getaway cottage by the sea, for a wonderful lunch. Unfortunately, the weather had deteriorated by then, and we were unable to roll Easter eggs on the beach.

Ethan's first Easter nest, or rather his initial spelled with daffodils. Under the cover of darkness, Cheryl and Barbara pinched these daffodils the previous night from nearby walking trails. Barbara's reputation in the community will never be the same. Here's the main man, posing with his goodies. He's a bit worn out from his photo shoot. Ethan showing a strong interest in purple flowers. We could not draw his attention away from them for several minutes!

A distant view of the Houston family in front of Mark and Barbara's house. You can even pick out the yellow 'E' on the grass at our feet! A more close-up family portrait, with Ethan's new Easter outfit.

Mark, Barbara, and Ethan waiting patiently while Cheryl and Chad pack. Alex and Ethan. Separated at birth? Ethan and Christine, who prepared our wondeful lunch.

Cheryl and Ethan running between the raindrops and jumping into the car, for the drive to the airport and return to Stavanger.
