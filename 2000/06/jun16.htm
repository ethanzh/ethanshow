Ethan would have been very disappointed if we didn't make it to the Willie Nelson outdoor concert in Stavanger. In honor of the red-headed stranger, Ethan wore his bandana. Surprisingly enough, Ethan was the only baby there!

The problem with outdoor summer concerts in Stavanger is that the summer part might not work out. And, when the temperature does drop a bit low, it does not help if one is wearing sandals. (Mom!) Things like that can cause a family to leave a show a bit early...
