Pictures from Cheryl's second ultrasound, September 21, 1999, Stavanger.

The first two photos are head shots, with the first much more clear than the second. What a good-looking baby! The third is a view of the little guy's femur. Lots of measurements are present on the final picture as well, if you can figure out what they are. "VKT 1839g" is an estimation of the weight. 1.8 kilos so far.
