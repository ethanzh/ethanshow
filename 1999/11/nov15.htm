What a little angel he was today. After yesterday's tantrums, he was quite quiet and content today. He managed to sleep through the two visitors of the day, and continued his pattern of waking up as soon as the visitors leave. People would be beginning to wonder if he has eyes, if not for these photos. Unfortunately, the end of the day brought the news that the jaundice was not going away. You know what that means--time to catch a few more rays! For 24 hours (except for feedings, of course). This time in a double-decker bilirubin bed with accompanying sunglasses. Mom's not too happy about this, but what else is there to do?
