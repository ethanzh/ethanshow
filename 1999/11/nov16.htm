Ethan writes his own column today.
    What a life! A guy just tries to get some sleep and people keep shaking him and poking him and trying to get him to wake up and suck a breast. It makes me kind of miss the old days, always floating in a warm, dreamy environment, with the food delievered automatically. No need to even make a phone call.

    Visitors keep streaming in, but I try to keep my eyes closed when they're around. At least today I finally got to meet a girl close to my own age. As you can see I was a bit shy--but hey, it was my first time! And, I had some trouble with the fact that she was about twice as big as I am. After the nice guests left I thought it was a good time to test out my new eyes and take a look around. I might be able to get used to this. And, by the way, what was that guy thinking when he bought those glasses?
